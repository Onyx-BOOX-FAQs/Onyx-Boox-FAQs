General Questions

ePaper, or electronic paper, is a display technology designed to mimic the appearance of real ink on paper. It reduces eye strain and is easier to read for long periods compared to traditional screens.

Onyx is the company behind BOOX devices. They specialize in E-Ink tablets and eReaders designed for reading, note-taking, and productivity.

Kindle is mainly focused on reading books from Amazon, while BOOX devices offer more flexibility with Android apps, note-taking, file support, and productivity features.

BOOX devices use E-Ink screens, which are easier on the eyes and provide better battery life. They are designed for reading, writing, and focused productivity rather than gaming or video-heavy use.
